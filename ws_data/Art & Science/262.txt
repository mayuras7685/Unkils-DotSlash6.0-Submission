The global pandemic caused by the novel coronavirus SARS-Cov-2 has changed the world and altered all of our lives. In this series of episodes titled “Scientist at Home” we present narratives of life and research during the pandemic from scientists and academics across a broad range of disciplines and stages of their careers. We hope in hearing these narratives you’ll feel a sense of camaraderie with the scientific community as we acclimate during these difficult times. Featured: Wei Li, A. Delphine Tripp, Edward Chen, Melis Tekant, Chad SteinProduced by: A.
